26th Putnam 1965


 
 
Problem A1

How many positive integers divide at least one of 1040 and 2030?

 

Solution

Answer: 2301.

Factors of 1040 have the form 2m5n with 0 <= m, n <= 40. So there are 412 = 1681 such factors. Factors of 2030 = 260530 not dividing 1040 have the form 2m5n with 41 ≤ m ≤ 60 and 0 ≤ n ≤ 30, so there are 20.31 = 620 such factors.
