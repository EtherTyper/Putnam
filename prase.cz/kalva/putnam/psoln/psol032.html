Problem A2

a1, a2, ... , an, b1, ... , bn are non-negative reals. Show that (∏ ai)1/n + (∏bi)1/n ≤ (∏(ai+bi))1/n.

 

Solution

If any ai or bi is zero, then the result is trivial, so assume all are positive. By AM/GM (∏ai/(ai+bi))1/n ≤ (∑ai/(ai+bi))/n. Similarly for bi. Add.
