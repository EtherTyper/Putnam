45th Putnam 1984


 
 
Problem A1

S is an a x b x c brick. T is the set of points a distance 1 or less from S. Find the volume of T.

 

Solution

Answer: abc + 2(ab + bc + ca) + π(a + b + c + 4/3).

Easy.

T comprises: S, volume abc; a slab on each face, two volume ab, two volume bc, two volume ca; a quarter cylinder on each edge, four with length a, total volume πa, four length b, total volume πb, and four length c, total volume πc; and an eighth sphere radius 1 at each vertex, total volume 4/3 π.
