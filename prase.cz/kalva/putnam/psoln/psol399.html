Problem B2

Evaluate   ∫13  ( (x - 1)(3 - x) )-1/2 dx   and   ∫1inf  (ex+1 + e3-x)-1 dx.

 

Solution

(1)   π. Trivial. [Write (x - 1)(3 - x) = 1 - (x - 2)2. So we have a standard sin-1z integral.]

(2)   π/(4 e2). Trivial. [Multiply top and bottom by ex. Change variable to y = ex-1. We now have a standard tan-1z integral.]
