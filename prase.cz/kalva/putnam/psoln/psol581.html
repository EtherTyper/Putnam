18th Putnam 1958


 
 
Problem A1

Show that the real polynomial ∑0n aixi has at least one real root if ∑ ai/(i + 1) = 0.

 

Solution

Integrate from 0 to 1. We get zero. Hence the polynomial has at least one zero between 0 and 1.
