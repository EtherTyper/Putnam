33rd IMO 1992 shortlist


 
 
Problem 16

Show that (5125 - 1)/(525 - 1) is composite.

 

Solution

Put x = 525. Then (5125-1)/(525-1) = x4+x3+x2+x+1 = (x2+3x+1)2 - 5x(x+1)2 which has factor (x2+3x+1-513x-513) which is obviously > 1 and < x4+x3+x2+x+1.
