Problem 1

p(x) is a quadratic polynomial with non-negative coefficients. Show that p(xy)2 ≤ p(x2)p(y2).

 

Solution

lhs = ( (√a x2)(√a y2) + (√b x)(√b y) + √c √c)2 ≤ (ax4 + bx2 + c)(ay4 + by2 + c) = rhs by Cauchy-Schwartz.
