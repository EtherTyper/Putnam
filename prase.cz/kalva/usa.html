From 1972 until 1995 each competition had a single paper of 5 questions (to be solved in 3 1/2 hours). Thereafter each competition has had two papers each with 3 questions (originally 3 hours for each paper, 4½ hours from 2002). So there are a total of 168 problems to date.

I have put up solutions for all problems.
