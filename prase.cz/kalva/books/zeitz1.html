Zeitz - comments


 
 

By Johannes Tang Lek Huo

This is actually a disappointment to many, I suppose. This book is very light on Geometry, which is an important part of Olympiad competitions. However, it gives the novice some insights into combinatorics. Its coverage of Number Theory is atrocious. And I have expected the book to define injective, surjective, bijective. The book is far too thin to give complete exposure to beginners.
