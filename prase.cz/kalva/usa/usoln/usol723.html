1st USAMO 1972


 
 
Problem 3

n digits, none of them 0, are randomly (and independently) generated, find the probability that their product is divisible by 10.

 

Solution

Answer: 1 - (8/9)n - (5/9)n + (4/9)n.

A number is divisible by 10 iff it has an even number and a 5 amongst its digits. The probability of no 5 is (8/9)n. The probability of no even number is (5/9)n. The probability of no 5 and no even number is (4/9)n. Hence result.
